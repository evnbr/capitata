Green public relations
and crisis management

In today’s hyper-connected world, crises spin out of control in seconds. An activist blogger, a savvy reporter, or a smartphone-enabled citizen can change your brand’s trajectory with a single tweet. When trouble arises, you need a response that will be remembered far longer than the crisis itself.

Overview

There are the monumental oil spills and diverse other energy-related disasters that mire companies in interminable scrutiny and litigation— and often a fight for their very lives. There are the nuclear mishaps that can panic communities and undo years of industry progress and acceptance. With front-page crises only a single incident away, the energy industry must be constantly prepared and ever vigilant. By immediately responding, companies of all sizes have the opportunity to earn the public’s trust and keep it.

What we do

We're there at your moment of truth. When lives are at risk. When liberty is at stake. When the future of your business is in jeopardy.

What drives us
